About Me

Hello, I'm Saikat Ghatak. I completed my undergraduate B.Sc. degree in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) in Dhaka, Bangladesh, where I discovered my passion for Artificial Intelligence, Human-Computer Interaction, and Generative AI. I’m especially excited about building AI that makes a real difference in people’s lives.

Currently, I’m a Research Assistant at BUET, exploring how Generative AI can make higher education more accessible for students with disabilities. Before this, I was a Research Intern at the eSystems Lab, where I worked on projects like the National Health Data Warehousing System and a Virtual Internship Platform.

Earlier in my career, I contributed as a Software Engineer at Kona Software Lab Ltd., developing secure fintech solutions such as a Mastercard-compliant Token Service Provider, API Gateway migration, and the OpenAPI Project.

Beyond research and engineering, I’m passionate about teaching and mentoring students in areas like Data Structures & Algorithms, Programming, and Databases. I’ve also been involved in robotics, sports, and community initiatives, which have shaped my journey as both a collaborator and a leader.
